With an increased interest in local and organically grown fresh produce, Produce Portal aims to make it easier for consumers to find locally grown fresh produce of their choice listed by small local farmers to large farms to individuals with backyard gardens. While an increased awareness of our food production and supply chain is encouraging, there is a shortage of effective tools to help trade this basic necessity locally, directly to consumers as well as to form a trusted network of local food growers and enthusiasts.

Our aim is to provide people with a tool to not only trade produce but to meet, interact and form a network of people interested in local food, urban farming, organic food, sustainable farming, etc. As more and more people become part of such a network, it will encourage farmers to sell more of their produce locally and directly to consumers and well as encourage smaller farmers and individuals to grow more food. Through such an involvement from the people, Produce Portal aims to become a place of agricultural knowledge exchange.

FAQ

How do I find local sellers?
Enter your zip code or city and optionally select the kind of produce you are looking for. The search will return with a list as well as a map view of local sellers.
How do I sell my produce?
Sign up as a seller and list your produce as well as price. Optionally include more details of your farming practices, bio, specialty of your farm, etc.
How do I interact and network with customers and sellers in my locality?
As you will be buying from the farmers, it's a great opportunity to interact with them directly. There is also an online forum where you can ask questions and share knowledge.
